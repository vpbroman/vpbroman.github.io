Vincent Broman family interests

VB's professional stuff used to be found at his work place, but policies changed, and some of it will appear here. This site has his spare-time and hobbyist materials. Send email to vbroman@sbcglobal.net, and if you are in his address book, the email won't be discarded as spam.

  • Chess software and games
  • The Greek New Testament and textual criticism
  • LifeLines report programs, from mailing list postings, intended for Tom Wetmore's LifeLines genealogy software on SourceForge. These are already in the distribution by now.
  • Fireside Chat slides and handouts.
  • V Broman GnuPG or PGP Keys

Most of the files on this site which are not HTML or RPM are stored in ZIPped form, with CRLFs, usually with one file per archive so that gzip can also handle it. If your mailcap maps .zip to something like zless or unzip -p, you should be able to see the files, not just download them. Reformatting the Greek material into Unicode is a backburner project.
